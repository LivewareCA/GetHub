Basic Granola

Information

  • TOTAL TIME: 0:30 PREP TIME: 0:05 COOK TIME: 0:25 SERVINGS: ?
INGREDIENTS QUANTITY
Oats 3 cups
Salt 1/2 teaspoon
Shredded coconut 4 tablespoons
Cinnamon 2 teaspoons
Oil 4 tablespoons
Vanilla 2 teaspoon
Honey 5 tablespoons
OPTIONAL FLAVOUR
Cocoa powder

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350ÂºF. Grease or line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the oats, salt, coconut, and cinnamon.
  3. Add in the oil, vanilla, and honey. Stir until oats are evenly coated.
  4. Evenly spread the granola onto the prepared baking sheet.
  5. Bake for about 15 minutes or until golden brown, stirring every few minutes so nothing burns.
  6. Let cool for a few minutes before transferring to an airtight container.
