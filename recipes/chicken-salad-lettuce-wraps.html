Chicken Salad Lettuce Wraps

Information

  • TOTAL TIME: 0:15 PREP TIME: 0:15 COOK TIME: 0 SERVINGS: 4
INGREDIENTS QUANTITY
Greek yogurt 1/2 cup
Lemon zest 1 teaspoon
Juice of lemons 1 1/2
Dijon mustard 1 teaspoon
Chopped Dill 2 tablespoons
Rotisserie chicken 3 cups (shredded)
Red grapes 1 cup (quartered)
Chopped pecans 1/2 cup
Green onions 4 (diced)
Lettuce leaves (large)

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, mix together Greek yogurt, lemon zest and juice, mustard, and dill, and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Stir in chicken, grapes, pecans, and green onions and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Spoon chicken salad into lettuce leaves, garnish with dill, and serve.
